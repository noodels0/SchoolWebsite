Our mission, as the Daniels Farm School Community, is to cultivate students as lifelong learners, capable of drawing upon personal and external resources to become productive, contributing community members and responsible world citizens. Dear DFS Families, 2021-22 DFS Kindergarten Orientation will take place on Wednesday, June 2, 2021. The parent orientation segment will take place in the morning, with two student visitation sessions scheduled for the afternoon. Specific details will be sent to all registered families in a few weeks. If you have not already registered your incoming kindergarten student, please do so using the following link: https://www.trumbullps.org/general/student-registration/kindergarten-registration.html If you have any questions, please contact the DFS front office at (203) 452-4388.
